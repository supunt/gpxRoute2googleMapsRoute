What is this tool ??

This is a tool to convert GPX (v1.1) files to segments of google maps urls that you can share.
Each segment will have a MAXIMUM of 25 way points. This is a google maps limitation
Once Converted, you can
1. Copy the maps url by clicking on
2. Open maps url on new tab by clicking on
3. Share maps url by clicking on
